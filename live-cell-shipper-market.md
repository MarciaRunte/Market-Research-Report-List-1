<p><p>The Live Cell Shipper market is segmented into Manual and Automatic Temperature-controlled systems. Manual Temperature-controlled shippers require human intervention to maintain optimal conditions, relying on ice packs or dry ice to regulate temperature throughout transportation. In contrast, Automatic Temperature-controlled shippers utilize advanced technology to continuously monitor and adjust temperature settings without manual input, ensuring consistent conditions during transit. Both types aim to preserve cell viability, but automatic systems offer improved reliability and ease of use for sensitive biological materials.</p></p>
