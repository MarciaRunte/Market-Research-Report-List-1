<p><p>A hybrid train is a type of locomotive that combines traditional diesel engines with alternative power sources, such as batteries or hydrogen fuel cells, to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions. These trains are designed to operate in a more environmentally friendly manner by utilizing regenerative braking systems, which capture and store energy during braking to power the train later. Hybrid trains are gaining popularity as the transportation sector seeks sustainable solutions to combat climate change and reduce dependency on fossil fuels.</p><p>The Hybrid Train Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period. The Asia-Pacific region is emerging as a key market due to rapid urbanization and investments in rail infrastructure.
