<p><p>The Nanofibrillar Cellulose market offers various production methods, each with distinct characteristics. The Acid Hydrolysis Method utilizes acid to break down cellulose, resulting in high yield but potential degradation of material. The Enzymatic Hydrolysis Method employs enzymes for a gentler process, yielding high purity and maintaining fiber integrity. The Electrospinning technique produces nanofibers through electric fields, enabling the creation of fine, uniform fibers suitable for advanced applications like nanocomposites and filtration, thus expanding market opportunities.</p></p>
