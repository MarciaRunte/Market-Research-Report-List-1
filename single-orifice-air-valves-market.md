<p><p>The Single Orifice Air Valves market consists of two main types: flanged and threaded valves. Flanged air valves feature a flat rim, allowing for secure connections to piping systems via bolted joints, ensuring a robust seal. Threaded air valves, on the other hand, use screw threads for connections, offering ease of installation and maintenance. Both types are essential in various applications for controlling air flow, preventing backflow, and enabling efficient operation in water supply and drainage systems.</p></p>
