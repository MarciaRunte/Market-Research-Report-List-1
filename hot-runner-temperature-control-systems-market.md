<p><strong>The Hot Runner Temperature Control Systems Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Gate Hot Runner System</li><li>Valve Gate Hot Runner System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot runner temperature control systems market focuses on improving injection molding efficiency by managing temperatures in open gate and valve gate hot runner systems. Open gate hot runner systems facilitate direct flow of molten plastic through gate locations, ensuring rapid cycle times and uniform fill. In contrast, valve gate hot runner systems use gates that seal off during filling, preventing drooling and allowing for precise control. Both systems enhance product quality, reduce waste, and optimize manufacturing processes across various industries.</p></p>
