<p><p>The optical position sensors market is categorized into three main types based on their measurement capabilities. One-Dimensional Optical Position Sensors are designed for detecting position along a single axis, ideal for applications requiring linear movement. Two-Dimensional Optical Position Sensors measure position across two axes, suitable for tasks like tracking surface movements. Multi-Axial Optical Position Sensors provide comprehensive tracking across multiple axes, offering enhanced precision and versatility for complex applications. These sensors are increasingly utilized in robotics, automation, and industrial applications.</p></p>
