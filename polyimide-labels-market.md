<p><p>Polyimide labels are available in two main types: continuous and die-cut. Continuous polyimide labels are supplied in rolls, allowing for high-speed printing and easy application, making them ideal for large-scale production and automation. In contrast, die-cut polyimide labels are pre-cut into specific shapes and sizes, offering precise application and versatility for various labeling needs. Both types are highly resistant to heat and chemicals, making them suitable for industries like electronics, aerospace, and automotive, where durability is essential.</p></p>
