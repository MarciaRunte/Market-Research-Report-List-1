<p><p>Anatomic pathology services encompass histopathology and cytopathology, both critical in diagnosing diseases. Histopathology involves examining tissue samples to identify abnormalities, aiding in cancer detection and monitoring. It typically involves biopsies and surgical specimens. Cytopathology, on the other hand, focuses on the analysis of individual cells from bodily fluids or smears, useful for early cancer detection and screening. Together, these services provide essential insights into patient health, guiding treatment decisions and improving outcomes in various medical fields.</p></p>
