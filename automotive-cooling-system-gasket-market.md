<p><strong>The Automotive Cooling System Gasket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Flange Gasket</li><li>Radiator Gasket</li><li>Exhaust Gasket</li><li>Water Pump Gasket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cooling system gasket market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Exhaust flange gaskets create a seal between exhaust components to prevent leaks. Radiator gaskets ensure effective sealing in the cooling system, preventing coolant leaks. Exhaust gaskets, positioned between the exhaust manifold and engine, maintain proper pressure and flow. Water pump gaskets, sealing the water pump to the engine, prevent coolant leakage. Each type is crucial for maintaining engine efficiency and overheating prevention.</p></p>
