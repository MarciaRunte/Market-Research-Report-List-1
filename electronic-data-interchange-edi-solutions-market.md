<p><p>Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) solutions facilitate the seamless exchange of business documents between organizations. The direct-coupling market involves direct connections between trading partners, allowing for real-time data transfer without intermediaries. This approach offers greater control and quicker transactions. In contrast, the Value-Added Network (VAN) market utilizes third-party networks to manage data exchanges, providing added services like data translation and security. While VANs can reduce the complexity of managing connections, they may introduce additional costs and potential latency in data transfer.</p></p>
