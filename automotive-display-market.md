<p><p>The automotive display market consists of various technologies, including TFT-LCD, PMLCD, and other display types. TFT-LCDs (Thin-Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Displays) offer high resolution and wide viewing angles, making them popular for dashboards and infotainment systems. PMLCDs (Passive Matrix LCDs) are typically less costly and used in simpler applications due to their lower power consumption and easier integration. Other display types include OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) and embedded displays, providing additional options for enhanced visual experiences in vehicles.</p></p>
