<p><p>The CBD oil market consists of three primary types: Broad Spectrum CBD oil, Full Spectrum CBD oil, and CBD Isolate. Broad Spectrum CBD oil contains a range of cannabinoids and terpenes except for THC, providing enhanced benefits without psychoactive effects. Full Spectrum CBD oil includes all cannabinoids, including trace amounts of THC, which may amplify its therapeutic properties through the entourage effect. CBD Isolate, on the other hand, contains pure CBD without any other cannabinoids or compounds, appealing to those seeking THC-free options.</p></p>
