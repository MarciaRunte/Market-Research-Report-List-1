<p><p>UV disinfection modules are categorized based on pressure types: low, medium, and high pressure. Low pressure modules emit UV light at lower intensity, ideal for water treatment, providing effective disinfection at longer wavelengths. Medium pressure modules generate a broader spectrum of UV light, delivering higher intensity for faster disinfection, suitable for larger volumes. High pressure modules produce a concentrated output for industrial applications, offering rapid pathogen inactivation. Each type serves specific needs in water, air, and surface disinfection.</p></p>
