<p><p>The thermal infrared sensors market consists of two main types: photon detection thermal infrared sensors and thermal detection thermal infrared sensors. Photon detection sensors operate by detecting the photons emitted by objects in the infrared spectrum, providing higher sensitivity and resolution for applications like spectroscopy and remote sensing. In contrast, thermal detection sensors measure the heat emitted by objects, ideal for applications such as surveillance and temperature monitoring. Both types are crucial for industries such as defense, aerospace, automotive, and industrial automation.</p></p>
