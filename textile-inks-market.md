<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Textile Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile inks are categorized based on their application and dye type. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fabrics, making them vibrant and durable. Acidic inks are primarily used on protein fibers like silk and wool, offering bright colors and excellent wash fastness. Paint inks provide opacity and are ideal for synthetic materials, while dispersion inks are used for printing on polyester fabrics through a process that requires heat to transfer dyes. Sublimation inks also target polyester, converting solid dye directly to gas for vibrant prints.</p></p>
