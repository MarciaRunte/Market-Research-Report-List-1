<p><p>The Salmonella Test Kit market comprises two primary types: PCR Detection Kits and Rapid Test Kits. PCR Detection Kits leverage polymerase chain reaction technology to identify Salmonella DNA with high sensitivity and specificity, making them suitable for laboratory settings. In contrast, Rapid Test Kits deliver quick results, often within hours, using immunological methods or enzyme-based detection. These kits are favored for on-site testing, offering convenience and speed, especially in food safety and quality control applications.</p></p>
