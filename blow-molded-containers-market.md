<p><p>Blow molded containers are produced using various plastics, each offering distinct properties for different applications. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is favored for its clarity and barrier properties, ideal for beverages. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is durable and resistant to chemicals, making it suitable for household products. Polypropylene is lightweight and flexible, used in containers for food and medical items. Polystyrene offers good insulation and is often used in food packaging. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile for various applications, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and low-cost. Polycarbonates are robust and heat-resistant, utilized in more specialized applications.</p></p>
