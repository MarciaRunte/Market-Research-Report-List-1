<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Imaging Market encompasses various types based on different luminescent phenomena. Chemical chemiluminescence involves reactions that emit light, primarily used in assays and diagnostics. Biochemical involves biological processes, such as bioluminescence from living organisms, often utilized in cellular and molecular studies. Electromagnetic refers to electroluminescence, where light is emitted through electrical phenomena, relevant in display technologies. Photons, in photoluminescence, are emitted following light absorption, commonly used in imaging applications and materials characterization. Each type serves unique applications across scientific and industrial sectors.</p></p>
