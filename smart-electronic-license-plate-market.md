<p><p>The Smart Electronic License Plate market comprises two main types: Electronic Tags and Monitoring Base Stations (Card Readers). Electronic Tags are affixed to vehicles, enabling real-time tracking and automated identification through RFID or other technologies. Monitoring Base Stations detect these tags, facilitating efficient toll collection, parking management, and traffic monitoring. Together, they enhance vehicle management and streamline urban mobility, providing data for smarter city planning while improving security and efficiency in vehicle-related processes.</p></p>
