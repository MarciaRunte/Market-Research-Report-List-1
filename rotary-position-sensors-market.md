<p><strong>The Rotary Position Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Rotary Position Sensors</li><li>Non-Contact Rotary Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary position sensors market is segmented into contact and non-contact types. Contact rotary position sensors use mechanical means, such as potentiometers, to measure angular position, often providing high accuracy and resolution but experiencing wear due to friction. In contrast, non-contact rotary position sensors employ technologies like magnetic, optical, or capacitive sensing, offering durability and longevity without wear, making them suitable for harsh environments. Both types are crucial in applications across automotive, industrial automation, and robotics.</p></p>
