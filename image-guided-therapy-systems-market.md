<p><p>The image-guided therapy systems market encompasses various imaging modalities essential for accurate diagnosis and minimally invasive treatments. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) delivers high-resolution images of soft tissues. Endoscopes enable visualization of internal structures. X-ray Fluoroscopy provides dynamic imaging for live monitoring. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) assess metabolic activities, enhancing therapeutic decisions.</p></p>
