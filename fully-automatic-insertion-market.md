<p><p>Fully automatic insertion markets encompass various types based on the orientation and form of components being inserted. Axial insertion targets parts like resistors or capacitors with leads running along the device axis, suitable for through-hole applications. Odd form insertion accommodates uniquely shaped or sized components not fitting standard configurations.
