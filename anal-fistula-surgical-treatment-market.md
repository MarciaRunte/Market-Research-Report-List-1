<p><p>The anal fistula surgical treatment market comprises several procedures used to manage this condition. Fistulotomy involves cutting open the fistula tract for healing. Bioprosthetic plugs utilize materials to close the fistula, promoting tissue regeneration. Advancement flap procedures involve relocating nearby tissue to cover the fistula, enhancing healing. Seton techniques use a suture to drain and keep the fistula open, allowing for gradual healing. Other methods may include laser therapies and injection treatments, offering various options for patient care.</p></p>
