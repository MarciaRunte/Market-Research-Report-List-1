<p><strong>The Rabies Vaccine Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-exposure Prophylaxis</li><li>Post-exposure Prophylaxis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rabies vaccine market encompasses two primary applications: pre-exposure prophylaxis (PrEP) and post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP). PrEP is administered to individuals at high risk of exposure, such as veterinarians or travelers to endemic areas, to build immunity before potential contact with the virus. PEP, on the other hand, involves vaccination after exposure to prevent the onset of rabies symptoms, following animal bites or potential encounters. Both applications are crucial for reducing rabies mortality and managing public health risks effectively.</p></p>
