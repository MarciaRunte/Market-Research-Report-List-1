<p><p>The PET plastic bottles recycling market consists primarily of two types: mechanical and chemical recycling. Mechanical recycling involves physically processing used PET bottles to create recycled pellets, which can be remolded into new products. This method is typically efficient but may degrade material quality over time. In contrast, chemical recycling breaks down PET into its molecular components, allowing for the restoration of original quality. This process can handle contaminated plastics, offering a broader recycling potential and enabling higher purity in recycled materials.</p></p>
