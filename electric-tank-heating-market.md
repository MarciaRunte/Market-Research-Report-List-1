<p><ul><li>Direct Heating</li><li>Indirect Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Tank Heating market can be categorized into two primary types: direct heating and indirect heating. Direct heating involves electric heating elements directly immersed in the liquid, providing efficient and rapid warmth. In contrast, indirect heating utilizes a heat exchanger, transferring heat from an electric source to the liquid without direct contact, ensuring safer and more controlled heating. Both methods cater to different industrial applications, balancing efficiency, safety, and heating speed to meet specific operational requirements.</p></p>
