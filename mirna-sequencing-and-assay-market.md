<p><p>miRNA sequencing involves analyzing microRNAs to understand their role in gene regulation and disease mechanisms. The market includes various sequencing technologies: Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS) uses fluorescently labeled nucleotides to detect DNA incorporation during synthesis; Nanopore sequencing allows real-time analysis by passing DNA through nanopores, detecting changes in current; and Ion Semiconductor Sequencing relies on detecting released hydrogen ions during nucleotide incorporation, allowing rapid sequencing. Each method offers unique benefits in accuracy, speed, and cost-effectiveness for miRNA research.</p></p>
