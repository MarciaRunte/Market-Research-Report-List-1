<p><p>Car navigation systems fall into three main market types: DVD-based, data-based, and GPS-based. </p><p>DVD-based systems rely on pre-loaded maps stored on DVDs, offering offline navigation but requiring manual updates. Data-based systems utilize online databases, allowing real-time updates and traffic information, ensuring up-to-date navigation but requiring internet connectivity. GPS-based systems leverage satellite signals for accurate positioning, providing turn-by-turn directions in real-time. Each type caters to different user needs, balancing convenience, accuracy, and technological demands in vehicle navigation.</p></p>
