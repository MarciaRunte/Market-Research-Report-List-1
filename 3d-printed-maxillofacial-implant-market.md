<p><p>The 3D printed maxillofacial implant market encompasses two primary types: internal fixators and external fixators. Internal fixators are devices implanted within the body to stabilize facial structures, aiding in reconstructive surgery and providing durability and precision. On the other hand, external fixators are mounted outside the body, offering stability to facial bones while allowing for adjustments post-surgery. Both types leverage 3D printing technology to create customized solutions that enhance patient outcomes and improve the overall effectiveness of maxillofacial treatments.</p></p>
