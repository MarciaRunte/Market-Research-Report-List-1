<p><p>Disposable external catheters are medical devices designed to collect urine from patients suffering from urinary incontinence or retention. These catheters are placed externally, typically adhering to the skin, and feature a drainage tube that directs urine into a collection bag. They provide a more comfortable and hygienic alternative to traditional indwelling catheters, reducing the risk of urinary tract infections and other complications.</p><p>The Disposable External Catheters Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increasing elderly population, rising awareness of urinary incontinence, and advancements in catheter technology. Various healthcare providers are emphasizing patient comfort and infection control, leading to a shift from traditional methods to more user-friendly options. The growing prevalence of urological disorders and a push for improved healthcare standards further bolster market demand.</p><p>Innovations such as skin-friendly adhesives, waterproof designs, and enhanced convenience features are emerging trends that contribute to the market's expansion.
