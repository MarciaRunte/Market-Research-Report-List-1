<p><strong>The Conductive Graphene Filament Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asphalt Based</li><li>Pan Based</li><li>Viscose Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive graphene filament market is segmented into three primary types: asphalt-based, pan-based, and viscose-based filaments. Asphalt-based filaments leverage the unique conductive properties of graphene enhanced with asphalt, suitable for various applications including flexible electronics. Pan-based filaments are derived from polyacrylonitrile and are known for their high tensile strength and conductivity, ideal for advanced composite materials. Viscose-based filaments utilize regenerated cellulose fibers, offering good conductivity while maintaining flexibility, making them suitable for wearable technologies and smart textiles.</p></p>
