<p><p>LNP substrates are vital in electronics and optoelectronics, comprising semiconducting and semi-insulating types. Semiconducting substrates, such as silicon or gallium arsenide, enable efficient charge transport, crucial for active device components like transistors and diodes. In contrast, semi-insulating substrates, often used in high-frequency applications, minimize charge carrier concentrations to prevent unintended conduction, enhancing device performance. The market for these substrates is driven by advancements in technology, including communications, computing, and renewable energy, demanding high-performance materials for innovative applications.</p></p>
