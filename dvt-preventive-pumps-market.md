<p><p>DVT preventive pumps are specialized devices designed to prevent deep vein thrombosis (DVT) by promoting blood circulation in the limbs. The market is segmented into lower extremity and upper extremity types. Lower extremity pumps target the legs and feet, delivering intermittent compression to enhance venous return and reduce clot risk, often used post-surgery or during long periods of immobility. Upper extremity pumps focus on the arms, typically utilized for patients at risk due to conditions affecting circulation or trauma.</p></p>
