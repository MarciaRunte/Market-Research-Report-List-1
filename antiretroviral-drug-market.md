<p><strong>The Antiretroviral Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Class Drugs Combination</li><li>NRTI</li><li>NNRTI</li><li>Protease Inhibitors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiretroviral drug market comprises various drug types targeting HIV treatment. Multi-class drug combinations enhance efficacy by utilizing multiple mechanisms to combat the virus. NRTIs (Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors) block viral replication by inhibiting reverse transcriptase, while NNRTIs (Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors) bind to the same enzyme but differently, disrupting its function. Protease inhibitors prevent viral maturation by inhibiting the protease enzyme. Additionally, other market segments include entry inhibitors and integrase inhibitors, further broadening treatment options for patients.</p></p>
