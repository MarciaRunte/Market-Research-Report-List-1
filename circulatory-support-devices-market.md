<p><strong>The Circulatory Support Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventricular Assisted Device (VAD)</li><li>Total Artificial Heart (TAH)</li><li>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circulatory support devices market includes several critical technologies. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) support heart function by aiding one or both ventricles, often used in heart failure patients awaiting transplants. Total Artificial Hearts (TAHs) completely replace a failing heart, providing temporary support until a donor heart is available. Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) serves as a life-saving solution for patients with severe cardiac and respiratory failure, allowing oxygenation of blood outside the body while supporting heart function.</p></p>
