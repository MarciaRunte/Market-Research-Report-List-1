<p><p>The ceiling fan market is primarily divided into two types: AC ceiling fans and DC ceiling fans. AC ceiling fans are powered by alternating current, offering reliable performance and a lower initial cost, making them popular in many households. In contrast, DC ceiling fans utilize direct current, providing higher energy efficiency, quieter operation, and advanced features like variable speed settings. As consumers increasingly prioritize energy conservation and technology integration, the demand for DC ceiling fans is steadily rising.</p></p>
