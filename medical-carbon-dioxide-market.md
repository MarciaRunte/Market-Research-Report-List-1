Liquid State CO2, stored under high pressure, is utilized for cryotherapy, tissue preservation, and sterilization. Both forms are essential in healthcare, with gaseous CO2 offering ease of use in clinical settings and liquid CO2 providing efficient storage and specialized applications.</p></p>
