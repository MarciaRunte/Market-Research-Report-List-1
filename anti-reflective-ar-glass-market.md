<p><p>The Anti-Reflective (AR) Glass market consists of several types based on the number of surfaces treated. Single Surface AR Glass features a double-layer coating that minimizes glare on one side, ideal for applications like eyewear. Double Surface AR Glass employs a four-layer coating, effectively reducing reflections on both sides, suitable for camera lenses and high-end displays. Multilayer AR Glass incorporates multiple coatings for enhanced performance, providing superior clarity and light transmission, making it ideal for advanced optical devices and architectural applications.</p></p>
