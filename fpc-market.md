<p><p>The flexible printed circuit (FPC) market consists of various circuit types catering to different applications. Single-sided circuits feature components on one side, making them cost-effective for simple devices. Double-sided circuits allow for components on both sides, enhancing circuit density. Multi-layer circuits incorporate multiple layers for complex configurations, ideal for advanced electronics. Rigid-flex circuits combine flexible and rigid materials, offering durability with flexibility, suitable for intricate designs in space-constrained environments like smartphones and medical devices.</p></p>
