<p><ul><li>Belt Type</li><li>Disc Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel mill skimmer market consists of two primary types: belt type and disc type. Belt type skimmers utilize a continuous conveyor belt to collect and remove slag and other impurities from molten metal, ensuring efficient system cleaning. In contrast, disc type skimmers employ rotating discs to skim the surface of molten material, effectively extracting debris. Both types enhance operational efficiency and reduce contamination, though their designs cater to varying operational needs and preferences within steel manufacturing processes.</p></p>
