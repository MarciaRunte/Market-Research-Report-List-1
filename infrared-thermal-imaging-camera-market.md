<p><p>The infrared thermal imaging camera market is divided into two main types: cooled and uncooled. Cooled infrared cameras utilize cryogenic cooling systems to reduce sensor temperature, enhancing sensitivity and image quality, making them ideal for high-performance applications like military and aerospace. In contrast, uncooled infrared cameras operate at ambient temperatures, using thermal sensors that are more cost-effective and compact, suitable for applications such as building inspections, firefighting, and automotive safety. Each type serves distinct industry needs based on performance and budget.</p></p>
