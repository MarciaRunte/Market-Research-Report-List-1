<p><p>The foreign body detectors market is segmented into three primary types: magnetic separators, metal detectors, and X-ray machines. Metal detectors identify metallic foreign objects using electromagnetic fields, ensuring product safety. X-ray machines provide a comprehensive inspection by detecting a variety of foreign bodies, including metal, glass, and plastic, through detailed imaging. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining product integrity and safety across various industries.</p></p>
