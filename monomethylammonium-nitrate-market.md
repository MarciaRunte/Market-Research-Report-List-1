<p><p>Monomethylammonium nitrate (MMAN) is utilized in various explosive formulations, notably water gel/slurry explosives, emulsion explosives, and seismic explosives. Water gel/slurry explosives combine ammonium nitrate with water and additives for enhanced stability and safety, making them suitable for underground applications. Emulsion explosives, featuring a water-resistant matrix, offer high energy and are ideal for wet conditions. Seismic explosives are specifically designed for geophysical applications, providing the necessary energy for subsurface exploration and assessment, thereby catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
