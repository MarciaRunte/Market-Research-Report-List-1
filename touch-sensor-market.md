<p><p>The touch sensor market comprises various types, including resistive and capacitive sensors, along with others like optical and hybrid sensors. Resistive sensors work by detecting pressure applied to the surface, suitable for stylus or gloved use. Capacitive sensors, more common in smartphones, sense changes in electrical charge when a conductive object, like a finger, approaches. Other types, such as optical sensors, utilize light to detect touch, enhancing versatility in applications ranging from consumer electronics to industrial equipment.</p></p>
