<p><p>The 3D automotive camera module market encompasses two primary sensor technologies: Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) and Charge-Coupled Device (CCD). CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and high integration capabilities, making them suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). In contrast, CCD sensors offer superior image quality with reduced noise and better light sensitivity, ideal for high-performance applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing safety, navigation, and overall driving experiences.</p></p>
