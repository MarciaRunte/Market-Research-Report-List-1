<p><p>Bi-metallic thermometers are temperature measurement devices that utilize the physical properties of two metal strips bonded together. As temperature changes, the differing expansion rates of the metals cause the strip to bend, which moves a pointer along a calibrated dial, indicating the temperature. These thermometers are commonly used in various industries, including HVAC, food processing, and manufacturing, due to their reliability, accuracy, and ease of use.</p><p>The Bi-Metallic Thermometers Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for accurate temperature measurement in diverse applications.
