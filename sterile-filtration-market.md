<p><p>The sterile filtration market comprises several membrane filter types, each suited for specific applications. MCE (Mixed Cellulose Ester) membrane filters are known for their efficient particle retention and are often used in microbiological analysis. Cellulose acetate membrane filters offer low protein binding, making them ideal for biotechnology. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) membrane filters possess excellent chemical resistance, suitable for aggressive solvents. Nylon membrane filters are versatile and compatible with various solvents. "Others" category includes specialized filters tailored for niche applications in sterilization processes.</p></p>
