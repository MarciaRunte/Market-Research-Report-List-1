Disc mulchers, on the other hand, utilize a series of circular blades for precise cutting and maneuverability, making them suitable for diverse terrains. The "others" category encompasses specialized mulchers like flail and chipper attachments, catering to niche applications and specific customer needs, enhancing versatility in land management.</p></p>
