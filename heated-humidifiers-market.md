<p><p>The heated humidifiers market comprises three main types: integrated humidifiers, stand-alone humidifiers, and built-in humidifiers. Stand-alone humidifiers operate independently, providing flexibility in placement and usage for individual rooms. Each type caters to different consumer needs, preferences, and space considerations.</p></p>
