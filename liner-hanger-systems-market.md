<p><strong>The Liner Hanger Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Liner Hanger Systems</li><li>Hydraulic Liner Hanger Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liner hanger systems are essential in oil and gas drilling, designed to suspend and seal liners in wellbores. Mechanical liner hanger systems utilize physical mechanisms, such as slips or wedges, to secure the liner in place during operations. In contrast, hydraulic liner hanger systems rely on fluid pressure to activate securing mechanisms, allowing for more precise control during installation. Both types cater to varying well conditions, providing flexibility and efficiency in managing downhole environments.</p></p>
