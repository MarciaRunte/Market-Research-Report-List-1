<p><p>The retroreflective films market is primarily categorized into two types: glass bead and micro-prism. Glass bead retroreflective films use embedded glass beads to reflect light back to its source, making them suitable for road signs and vehicles. In contrast, micro-prism retroreflective films employ a prism structure to enhance light return efficiency, offering superior visibility in various lighting conditions. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing safety and visibility in traffic signage, apparel, and industrial applications.</p></p>
