<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Cooled</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor chiller market is primarily divided into water-cooled and air-cooled types. Water-cooled chillers utilize water as a cooling medium, providing efficient heat removal and often requiring a cooling tower for heat dissipation. They are ideal for larger facilities with higher cooling demands. In contrast, air-cooled chillers use ambient air to dissipate heat and are generally easier to install and maintain, making them suitable for smaller operations or locations with limited water access. Both types are essential for effective temperature control in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
