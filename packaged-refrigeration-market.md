<p><strong>The Packaged Refrigeration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Pipe System</li><li>Three Pipe System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packaged refrigeration systems are categorized primarily into two types: double pipe and three pipe systems. The double pipe system consists of two concentric pipes, where one carries refrigerant and the other provides water for cooling, facilitating effective temperature exchange. On the other hand, the three pipe system includes an additional pipe that allows simultaneous heating and cooling, enhancing efficiency. Both systems serve commercial and industrial applications, varying in complexity, cost, and suitability based on specific cooling and heating needs.</p></p>
