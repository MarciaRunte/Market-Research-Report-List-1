<p><p>The HVAC Repair, Maintenance, and Installation market consists of two main types: centralized and distributed HVAC maintenance. Centralized HVAC maintenance focuses on managing a single, central system that serves multiple zones or buildings, providing uniform temperature control and energy efficiency. In contrast, distributed HVAC maintenance involves multiple independent systems located throughout a building or facility, allowing for localized control and flexibility. Both approaches aim to enhance system performance, reduce downtime, and ensure occupant comfort while lowering operating costs.</p></p>
