<p><ul><li>Air Cooler</li><li>Water Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPU cooler market primarily consists of air and water coolers. Air coolers rely on heat sinks and fans to dissipate heat from the CPU, offering an affordable and low-maintenance solution. They are suitable for average users and gaming enthusiasts. Water coolers use liquid coolant to absorb and transfer heat away from the CPU, providing efficient cooling for high-performance systems. Though typically more expensive, they enable quieter operation and superior thermal management, making them ideal for overclocking and demanding applications.</p></p>
