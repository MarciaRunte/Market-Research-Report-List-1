<p><p>The smoke alarm system market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Photoelectric Smoke Alarm System**: Utilizes a light source to detect smoke through light scattering, ideal for smoldering fires.</p><p> </p><p>2. **Ionization Smoke Alarm System**: Employs ionization technology to sense fast-flaming fires, providing rapid detection of flames. </p><p>3. **Combination Smoke Alarm System**: Integrates both photoelectric and ionization technologies, ensuring comprehensive protection against different types of fires for enhanced safety and reliability.</p></p>
