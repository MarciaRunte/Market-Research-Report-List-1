<p><p>The industrial refrigeration systems market comprises key components such as compressors, condensers, and evaporators. Compressors are responsible for circulating refrigerant and increasing its pressure, facilitating the cooling process. Condensers convert refrigerant gas into liquid by dissipating heat, while evaporators absorb heat from the environment, creating a cooling effect. These components work together to maintain optimal temperatures in various applications, such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical manufacturing, thereby ensuring product preservation and operational efficiency.</p></p>
