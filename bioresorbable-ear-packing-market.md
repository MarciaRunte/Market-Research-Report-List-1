<p><p>The bioresorbable ear packing market is categorized into two primary types: middle ear and outer ear. Middle ear products are designed for surgeries or conditions affecting the tympanic cavity, promoting healing while being gradually absorbed by the body. Outer ear products, on the other hand, cater to issues like ear canal injuries or post-operative care, providing support and protection while dissolving over time. Both types aim to enhance patient recovery and comfort, reducing the need for follow-up removal procedures.</p></p>
