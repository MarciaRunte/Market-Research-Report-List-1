<p><p>The Heavy Commercial Vehicle Transmission Fluids market consists primarily of two types: Manual Transmission Fluids (MTF) and Automatic Transmission Fluids (ATF). MTF is designed for manual gear systems, providing lubrication, reducing wear, and facilitating smooth gear shifting. In contrast, ATF is formulated for automatic transmissions, enabling precise hydraulic control and enhancing performance under varying temperatures. Both fluid types are critical for maintaining vehicle efficiency, prolonging component life, and ensuring reliable operation in demanding commercial environments.</p></p>
