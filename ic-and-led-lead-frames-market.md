<p><p>The IC and LED lead frames market consists of various package types, each tailored for specific applications. DIP (Dual In-line Package) features two parallel rows of pins, ideal for through-hole mounting. SOP (Small Outline Package) and SOT (Small Outline Transistor) are surface-mount packages offering space efficiency. QFP (Quad Flat Package) provides a larger number of pins for complex circuits. DFN (Dual Flat No-lead) and QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) offer a compact design. FC (Flip Chip) utilizes solder bumps for direct bonding, while TO (Transistor Outline) is suited for power devices. Others include specialized or custom packages.</p></p>
