<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Packaging CDMO market encompasses two main segments: primary packaging and secondary packaging. Primary packaging involves direct contact with the product, often serving functions like protection, preservation, and branding. Examples include bottles, vials, and blister packs. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, groups primary packages for distribution and sale, enhancing logistics and presentation. This includes boxes, cartons, and labels, focusing on product visibility and protection during transport. Both types are crucial for effective product management and consumer appeal.</p></p>
