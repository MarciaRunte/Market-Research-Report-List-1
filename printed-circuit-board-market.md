<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market comprises various types catering to different applications. IC substrates serve as foundations for integrated circuits, and flexible circuits enable lightweight, adaptable designs. Rigid-flex boards combine rigidity with flexibility, suitable for compact spaces. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialty designs, addressing niche requirements across diverse industries.</p></p>
