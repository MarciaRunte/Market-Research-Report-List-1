<p><ul><li>Long Fiber</li><li>Short Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modal fiber market is primarily divided into two types: long fiber and short fiber. Long fiber modal is characterized by its continuous strands, offering enhanced strength, softness, and a luxurious feel, making it ideal for high-end textiles and apparel. In contrast, short fiber modal consists of shorter strands, often used in products where bulk and volume are prioritized, such as non-woven fabrics and low-cost textile applications. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs in the textile industry.</p></p>
