<p><ul><li>ABS Material</li><li>PC Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow helmet market is primarily segmented into two material types: ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) and PC (Polycarbonate). ABS helmets are known for their affordability and durability, making them a popular choice for casual users. In contrast, PC helmets are lighter and offer better impact resistance, appealing to more serious skiers and snowboarders. Both materials cater to different consumer needs, with ABS focusing on cost-effectiveness and PC prioritizing enhanced safety and performance, thus shaping their respective market segments.</p></p>
