<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Non-woven Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Laid Type</li><li>Spunmelt Type</li><li>Wet-Laid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-woven fabrics market includes three primary types: Dry-Laid, Spunmelt, and Wet-Laid. </p><p>Dry-Laid involves laying fibers in a uniform layer and bonding them through mechanical or thermal methods, suitable for products like disposable hygiene items. </p><p>Spunmelt combines spunbond and meltblown processes, creating lightweight, versatile materials often used in medical and filtration applications. </p><p>Wet-Laid uses a water-based solution to deposit fibers, resulting in softer fabrics ideal for hygiene, wipes, and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific functional and performance needs.</p></p>
