<p><p>The anti-static plastic additives market is primarily divided into two types: coating and internal additives. Coating type additives are applied as a surface treatment, providing temporary anti-static properties, ideal for applications where static build-up is a concern but is not permanent. Internal additives, on the other hand, are blended into the plastic during manufacturing, offering a long-lasting solution by modifying the material's conductivity throughout its lifecycle, making them suitable for various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
