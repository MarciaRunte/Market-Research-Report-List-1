<p><p>The Compact Industrial PC market is primarily divided into two types: fanless and fanned systems. Fanless Compact Industrial PCs are designed for energy efficiency and low noise operation, making them ideal for environments with dust or restricted airflow. They utilize thermal management techniques to dissipate heat passively. In contrast, fanned Compact Industrial PCs employ internal fans to enhance cooling, enabling higher performance levels and supporting more demanding applications. Both types cater to varied industrial needs, ensuring reliability and durability in challenging conditions.</p></p>
