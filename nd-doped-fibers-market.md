<p><p>Nd-doped fibers are utilized in laser applications, categorized into two primary types: single-clad and double-clad fibers. Single-clad fibers feature a simple structure with one cladding, offering ease of fabrication and integration, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, double-clad fibers have an additional outer cladding layer, enhancing efficient pump light absorption and improving overall laser performance. This design is ideal for high-power laser systems, providing better brightness and output efficiency, appealing to specialized industrial and medical uses.</p></p>
