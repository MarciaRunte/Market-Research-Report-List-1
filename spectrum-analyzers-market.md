<p><p>The spectrum analyzers market is categorized into three main types: Swept-Tuned, Vector Signal, and Real-Time analyzers. Swept-Tuned analyzers measure signal power across a range of frequencies sequentially, offering good sensitivity but slower response. Vector Signal analyzers provide detailed information on modulation formats, enabling complex signal analysis. Real-Time analyzers continuously capture and analyze signals, providing immediate insights into transient events and faster signals, making them valuable for modern communication and testing applications in various industries.</p></p>
