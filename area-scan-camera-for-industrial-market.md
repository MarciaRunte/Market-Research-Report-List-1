<p><p>Area scan cameras are pivotal in the industrial market for machine vision applications, capturing images of entire fields in a single exposure. Two primary technologies dominate this segment: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS cameras are known for their faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and integrated features, making them ideal for high-speed applications. In contrast, CCD cameras offer superior image quality and low noise, making them preferable for precision applications where clarity is paramount.</p></p>
