<p><p>The Signal & Data Cables market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into Twisted Pair, Coaxial, and Optical Fiber cables. Twisted Pair cables, utilizing copper, are widely used in telecommunications for data transmission, while Coaxial cables, also copper-based, are popular for television and internet connectivity due to their shielding against interference. Optical Fiber cables, made of glass or plastic fibers, offer high-speed data transmission over long distances with minimal signal loss, making them essential for advanced networking and telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
