<p><p>The explosion-proof industrial vacuum cleaners market comprises two main types: electric and pneumatic vacuum cleaners. Electric industrial vacuum cleaners use electric power to operate, providing efficient suction and mobility for various hazardous environments. Pneumatic industrial vacuum cleaners, on the other hand, rely on compressed air, offering advantages in environments where electricity poses risks, such as flammable materials. Both types are designed to mitigate explosion risks while effectively collecting dust, debris, and hazardous materials from industrial settings, ensuring safety and compliance.</p></p>
