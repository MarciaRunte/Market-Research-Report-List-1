<p><p>Photomask defect detection equipment encompasses advanced technologies used in the semiconductor manufacturing process to ensure the integrity of photomasks. Photomask substrate inspection equipment focuses on identifying defects in the mask substrate itself, ensuring that the foundation for circuit design is flawless. In contrast, photomask pattern detection equipment targets defects within the patterned areas of the photomask, verifying that the intricate designs are accurately reproduced. Both types are essential for maintaining high quality and yield in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
