<p><p>Water-borne protective coatings are environmentally friendly coatings that use water as the primary solvent for dispersion and application. These coatings are widely used in various industries, including construction, automotive, and furniture, due to their low volatile organic compound (VOC) content, quick drying time, and ease of application. They offer excellent durability, adhesion, and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for protecting surfaces from environmental damage.</p><p>The Water-borne Protective Coatings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.9% during the forecast period.
