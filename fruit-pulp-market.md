<p><p>The fruit pulp market is categorized into three main types: berry fruit, exotic fruit, and orchard fruit. Exotic fruit pulp includes less common varieties such as mangoes and passion fruit, appealing for their unique tastes and health benefits. Orchard fruit pulp comes from traditional fruits like apples and pears, commonly utilized in sauces, jams, and baking products.</p></p>
