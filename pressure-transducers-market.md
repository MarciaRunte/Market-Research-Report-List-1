<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Pressure Transducers</li><li>Capacitive Pressure Transducers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure transducer market comprises various technologies, primarily including piezoresistive and capacitive pressure transducers. Piezoresistive pressure transducers operate by utilizing the changes in electrical resistance of a material when subjected to mechanical stress, making them ideal for accurate measurements in harsh environments. Capacitive pressure transducers measure pressure by detecting changes in capacitance caused by the displacement of a diaphragm under pressure. Both types serve diverse applications across industries, including automotive, aerospace, and healthcare, due to their reliability and accuracy.</p></p>
