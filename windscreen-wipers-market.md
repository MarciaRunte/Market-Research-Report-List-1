<p><p>The windscreen wiper market includes boneless and bone wipers. Boneless wipers feature a sleek, one-piece design with a flexible rubber blade, offering better aerodynamics, reduced noise, and improved contact with the windshield. They are durable and perform well in harsh weather. Bone wipers, or traditional wipers, have a metal frame supporting the rubber blade. They are cost-effective and widely used but may struggle with uneven pressure distribution. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, balancing performance, durability, and affordability.</p></p>
