<p><p>Forearm crutches, also known as elbow crutches, are assistive devices designed to provide mobility support to individuals with temporary or permanent mobility impairments. The market for forearm crutches is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing aging population, rising incidence of mobility-related disorders, and a growing emphasis on rehabilitation and patient independence.</p><p>The Forearm Crutches Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.90% during the forecast period.
