<p><p>The strain gauge sensor market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Resistive strain gauges work by changing resistance proportional to deformation. Semiconductor types utilize silicon materials for higher sensitivity but may have a narrower temperature range. Piezoresistive gauges alter their resistance under mechanical stress, enhancing accuracy. Inductive strain gauges rely on changes in inductance due to strain. Other types include optical and capacitive gauges, expanding market diversity for various industrial, automotive, and aerospace applications.</p></p>
