<p><p>The oocyte aspiration pumps market is categorized into single vacuum aspiration pumps and dual vacuum aspiration pumps. Single vac aspiration pumps utilize a single vacuum source, allowing for efficient retrieval of oocytes during procedures. These are typically more compact and cost-effective. In contrast, dual vac aspiration pumps employ two vacuum sources, providing enhanced control over suction strength and flow, which can improve oocyte retrieval success rates and reduce tissue trauma. Both types cater to varying clinical needs and preferences in reproductive medicine.</p></p>
