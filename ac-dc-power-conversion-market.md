<p><p>The AC-DC power conversion market is divided into two main types: external AC-DC power and embedded AC-DC power. External AC-DC power solutions involve standalone power adapters or converters that convert alternating current from the wall outlet to direct current for various devices. These are commonly used in consumer electronics. Embedded AC-DC power refers to power supplies integrated within devices, providing efficient power management directly onboard, such as in medical equipment or industrial machines, enhancing reliability and space efficiency.</p></p>
