<p><p>The molecular modeling market comprises various approaches, primarily categorized into molecular mechanics and quantum chemistry. The molecular mechanics approach employs classical physics principles to simulate molecular interactions and dynamics, focusing on potential energy calculations for large systems. In contrast, the quantum chemistry approach utilizes quantum mechanical principles to provide detailed insights into electronic structures and properties at the atomic level, making it suitable for smaller systems or reactions. Both methods are essential for drug design, materials science, and biochemical research.</p></p>
