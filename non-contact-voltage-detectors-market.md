<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Magnetic Induction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact voltage detectors are essential tools in the electrical industry, operating primarily through two types: capacitive and magnetic induction. Capacitive detectors sense the electric field generated around live wires, allowing users to identify voltage presence from a safe distance. Magnetic induction detectors, on the other hand, detect the magnetic field produced by current flow in conductors. Both technologies enhance safety by enabling users to ascertain voltage without direct contact, thus reducing the risk of electric shock during maintenance and troubleshooting tasks.</p></p>
