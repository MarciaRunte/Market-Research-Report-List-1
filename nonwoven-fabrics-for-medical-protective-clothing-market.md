<p><p>The nonwoven fabrics market for medical protective clothing includes various types that cater to specific protective needs. SMS nonwoven fabrics consist of a layer of spunbond material sandwiched between two layers of melt-blown material, offering superior barrier properties. SMMS introduces another spunbond layer for added strength and filtration. SMMMS further enhances this by adding another melt-blown layer for improved protection. Other nonwoven types may include composites or specialty fabrics designed for specific applications in the medical field, ensuring optimal safety and comfort.</p></p>
