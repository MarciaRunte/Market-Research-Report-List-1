<p><p>The automotive brake friction product market comprises several key components: brake pads, brake shoes, and brake linings. Brake pads are crucial for disc braking systems, providing friction against rotors. Brake shoes are used in drum brakes, expanding to press against the drum for effective stopping. Brake linings are materials affixed to brake shoes or drums that absorb and transfer heat during braking, enhancing performance and safety. Together, these components ensure reliable braking performance across various vehicle types.</p></p>
