<p><p>Lacquered HDF (High-Density Fiberboard) is available in two primary market types: single-faced and double-faced. Single-faced lacquered HDF features a glossy finish on one side, making it ideal for applications where only one side is visible, such as wall panels or decorative surfaces. In contrast, double-faced lacquered HDF has a polished finish on both sides, offering versatility for furniture and cabinetry where both surfaces may be exposed. This differentiation caters to various aesthetic and functional needs in the interior design market.</p></p>
