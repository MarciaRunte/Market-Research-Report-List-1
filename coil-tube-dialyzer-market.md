<p><strong>The Coil Tube Dialyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coil tube dialyzer market can be categorized into two main types: low flux membrane and high flux membrane dialyzers. Low flux membranes are designed to remove smaller toxins and waste products from the blood, typically used in patients with stable kidney function. In contrast, high flux membranes allow for the removal of larger molecules and a more extensive array of toxins, making them suitable for patients with more severe renal impairment. Each type caters to specific clinical needs in dialysis treatment.</p></p>
