<p><strong>The Oxygen Gas Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Oxygen Sensors</li><li>Amperometric Oxygen Sensors</li><li>Resistive Oxygen Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen gas sensors market comprises three main types: potentiometric, amperometric, and resistive sensors. Potentiometric oxygen sensors measure voltage changes related to oxygen concentration, providing accurate readings in varying conditions. Amperometric sensors detect oxygen levels by measuring the current generated during a redox reaction, making them suitable for dynamic environments. Resistive oxygen sensors evaluate changes in electrical resistance as oxygen levels vary, offering simplicity and robustness. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like automotive, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
