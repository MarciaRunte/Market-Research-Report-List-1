<p><strong>The Graphics Display Terminal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Raster</li><li>Vector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphics display terminal market encompasses two primary types: raster and vector displays. Raster displays, also known as bitmap displays, render images as a grid of pixels, making them ideal for detailed visuals like photographs. Conversely, vector displays represent images through geometric shapes defined by mathematical equations, ensuring scalability and clarity at any resolution, making them suitable for graphics like logos and diagrams. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to diverse needs in design, gaming, and scientific visualization.</p></p>
