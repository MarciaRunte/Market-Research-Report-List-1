<p><p>The Airborne LiDAR system market is primarily categorized into two types: Topographic LiDAR and Bathymetric LiDAR. Topographic LiDAR utilizes laser sensors to capture land surface features and elevations, making it essential for applications like forestry, urban planning, and environmental monitoring. In contrast, Bathymetric LiDAR penetrates water surfaces to measure underwater topography, crucial for coastal management, marine habitat mapping, and navigation. Both types leverage advanced technology to provide precise and detailed spatial data for various industries.</p></p>
