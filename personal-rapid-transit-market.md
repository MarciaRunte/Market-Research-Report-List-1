<p><ul><li>Vehicle Switching</li><li>Track Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Personal Rapid Transit (PRT) systems can be categorized into two primary market types: vehicle switching and track switching. Vehicle switching involves passenger pods that can change between different lines or routes while remaining on the same track, enhancing efficiency and flexibility. In contrast, track switching allows individual pods to move seamlessly from one track to another, optimizing route connectivity and reducing travel times. Both systems aim to improve urban mobility and provide convenient, on-demand transportation options.</p></p>
