<p><p>The PA hot melt adhesive market consists primarily of two types: powder and granule forms. Powdered PA adhesives offer precise application, allowing for controlled coverage and minimal waste, making them ideal for intricate designs and detailed work. In contrast, granule adhesives provide ease of handling and application, melting quickly when heated, which is beneficial for larger-scale manufacturing processes. Both types are widely used across industries such as packaging, automotive, and textiles, catering to diverse bonding needs.</p></p>
