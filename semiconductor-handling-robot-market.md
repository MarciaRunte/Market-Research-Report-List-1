<p><p>The semiconductor handling robot market includes two primary types: vacuum robotic and atmospheric robotic systems. Vacuum robotic systems utilize controlled environments to minimize contamination and ensure precision during the handling of delicate semiconductor materials. In contrast, atmospheric robotic systems operate in standard atmospheric conditions, focusing on cost-effectiveness and versatility for less sensitive applications. Both types cater to the growing demand for automation in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing efficiency, accuracy, and safety in the production process.</p></p>
