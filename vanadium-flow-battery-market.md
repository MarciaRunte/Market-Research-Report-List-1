In contrast, Graphite Felt Electrodes offer superior flexibility and surface area, enhancing energy storage capacity and charge/discharge efficiency. These electrode materials significantly influence the overall performance, energy density, and lifespan of Vanadium Flow Batteries, making them essential components in energy storage solutions for renewable energy integration and large-scale applications.</p></p>
