<p><strong>The In-Mold Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding In-Mold Labels</li><li>Blow Molding In-Mold Labels</li><li>Thermoforming IML Processes In-Mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Mold Labels (IML) market comprises three main types based on molding techniques: </p><p>1. **Injection Molding IML**: Labels are integrated into plastic during the injection molding process, providing high durability and aesthetic appeal for containers and products.</p><p>2. **Blow Molding IML**: This method involves applying the label during the blow molding of hollow objects, ideal for large containers like bottles.</p><p>3. **Thermoforming IML Processes**: Used for thin-walled products, this involves placing the label within heated molds before shaping the plastic, ensuring efficient production and excellent label adhesion.</p></p>
