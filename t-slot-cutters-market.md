<p><p>T-slot cutters are specialized tools used in machining to create T-shaped grooves in materials. The market for T-slot cutters is categorized into two primary types based on their shank designs: taper shank and straight handle. Taper shank cutters feature a conical shank that allows for better grip in milling machines, providing stability during operations. In contrast, straight handle cutters have a uniform diameter, making them suitable for standard milling applications. Both types cater to diverse machining needs, influencing market demand.</p></p>
