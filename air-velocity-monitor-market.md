<p><p>The air velocity monitor market is divided into two main types: analog and digital air velocity monitors. Analog air velocity monitors use mechanical components to measure air speed, providing readings through gauges or dials. They are often simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, digital air velocity monitors utilize sensors and electronic displays for precise measurements, offering advanced features such as data logging and connectivity options. Both types serve essential roles in HVAC, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
