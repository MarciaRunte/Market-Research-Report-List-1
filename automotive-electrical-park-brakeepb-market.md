<p><p>The Automotive Electrical Park Brake (EPB) market is segmented into two types: **With Auto Hold** and **Without Auto Hold**. EPB with Auto Hold automatically engages the brake when the vehicle stops, preventing rollback on inclines and enhancing convenience in stop-and-go traffic. EPB without Auto Hold requires manual activation, offering basic parking brake functionality. Both systems improve safety and space efficiency, but the Auto Hold feature adds advanced driver assistance, making it more appealing in modern vehicles for enhanced comfort and control.</p></p>
