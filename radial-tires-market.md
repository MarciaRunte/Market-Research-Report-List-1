<p><p>Radial tires are designed with layers of fabric cords running perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better stability, fuel efficiency, and durability. **All-steel radial tires** use steel belts and cords, making them robust and ideal for heavy-duty vehicles. **Semi-steel radial tires** combine steel belts with fabric cords, balancing strength and flexibility for medium-duty applications. **Full-fiber radial tires** rely entirely on synthetic or natural fibers, providing lightweight and smooth performance, often used in passenger vehicles. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing strength, weight, and cost.</p></p>
