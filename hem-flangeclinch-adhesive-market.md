<p><p>The Hem Flange (Clinch) Adhesive market is categorized into one-component and two-component systems. One-component adhesives are ready to use, requiring no mixing, and cure upon exposure to air or heat, offering convenience and ease of application. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed prior to application, providing superior bond strength and durability. Both types are essential in automotive and industrial applications for enhancing structural integrity and ensuring longevity of bonded joints.</p></p>
