<p><ul><li>IGBT</li><li>MOSFET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Integrated Modules (PIMs) encompass various market types, primarily focusing on IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) and MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) technologies. IGBTs are favored for high-power applications, offering efficient switching and thermal performance, making them ideal for applications like motor drives and renewable energy systems. Conversely, MOSFETs are preferred in lower power and high-speed applications due to their faster switching capabilities and lower conduction losses, making them suitable for consumer electronics and automotive applications. Both play crucial roles in modern power electronics, impacting efficiency and performance across various industries.</p></p>
