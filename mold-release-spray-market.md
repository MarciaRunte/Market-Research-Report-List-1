<p><p>The mold release spray market consists of two main types: silicone mold release spray and silicone-free mold release spray. Silicone mold release sprays contain silicone compounds that provide excellent lubrication and a smooth release from molds, making them ideal for applications in plastics, rubber, and composites. In contrast, silicone-free mold release sprays are formulated without silicone and are preferred in industries where silicone contamination must be avoided, such as food processing or volatile electronics, offering effective release properties while ensuring compatibility with various substrates.</p></p>
