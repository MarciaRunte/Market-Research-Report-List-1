<p><strong>The Chemical Vortex Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Vortex Pump</li><li>Open Vortex Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical vortex pump market includes two main types: closed vortex pumps and open vortex pumps. Closed vortex pumps feature a sealed impeller design that enhances efficiency and minimizes the risk of contamination, making them ideal for handling corrosive chemicals. In contrast, open vortex pumps utilize a more exposed impeller design, allowing for versatile flow handling but often with decreased efficiency compared to closed types. Both types cater to specific industrial needs based on fluid dynamics and chemical compatibility.</p></p>
