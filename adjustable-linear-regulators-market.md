<p><p>The adjustable linear regulators market comprises two main types: single-channel and multi-channel regulators. Single-channel regulators provide a single output voltage, often used in simpler applications requiring stable power. In contrast, multi-channel regulators can deliver multiple outputs at different voltage levels, making them ideal for complex systems demanding various voltage supplies. Both types are essential for efficient power management in electronic devices, with single-channel regulators being preferred for straightforward applications and multi-channel regulators suited for intricate designs with multiple components.</p></p>
