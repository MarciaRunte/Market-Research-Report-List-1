<p><p>The car dashcam market includes single-channel and multi-channel dashcams. Single-channel dashcams record footage from one camera, typically the front view, offering basic recording for accidents or road incidents. Multi-channel dashcams support multiple cameras, capturing front, rear, and sometimes interior views, providing comprehensive coverage for enhanced security and evidence. Single-channel models are cost-effective and simple, while multi-channel dashcams cater to users needing detailed monitoring, such as fleet operators or ride-sharing drivers, ensuring broader situational awareness and protection.</p></p>
