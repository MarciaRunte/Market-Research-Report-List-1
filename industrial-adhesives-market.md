<p><p>The industrial adhesives market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and commonly used in packaging and woodworking. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding and are ideal for demanding conditions. Hot-melt adhesives heat to apply and cool to set, suitable for quick bonding in packaging and assembly. Pressure-sensitive adhesives provide instant tack and are widely utilized in labels and tapes. Other adhesives include structural and reactive types, catering to niche applications across various industries.</p></p>
