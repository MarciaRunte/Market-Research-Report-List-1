<p><strong>The Liquid Transfer Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Transfer Pump</li><li>DC Transfer Pump</li><li>Hand Transfer Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid transfer pumps market is categorized into three main types: AC transfer pumps, DC transfer pumps, and hand transfer pumps. AC transfer pumps are typically used for stationary applications, offering high efficiency for larger volumes, often powered by electrical mains. DC transfer pumps are more portable, suitable for mobile applications, and can be powered by batteries, making them ideal for remote use. Hand transfer pumps, on the other hand, are manually operated, providing a simple and cost-effective solution for transferring liquids without electricity.</p></p>
