<p><p>The IO Link sensors market encompasses various types, including inductive sensors, capacitive sensors, photoelectric sensors, and others. Inductive sensors detect metallic objects through electromagnetic fields, making them ideal for automation. Capacitive sensors sense changes in capacitance, suitable for detecting non-metallic materials. Photoelectric sensors use light beams to detect the presence or absence of objects, widely used in packaging and logistics. The "others" category may include specialized sensors, like magnetic or ultrasonic sensors, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
