<p><p>Ballast Water Management Systems (BWMS) are critical technologies used in ships to manage and treat ballast water, preventing the introduction of invasive aquatic species into new marine environments. Companies are increasingly focusing on innovative solutions, including eco-friendly chemical treatments and advanced filtration techniques. Furthermore, the growing trend of retrofitting existing vessels with BWMS is expected to fuel the market.
