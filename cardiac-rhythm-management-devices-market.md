<p><p>Cardiac Rhythm Management Devices encompass various therapeutic technologies, primarily focusing on Implantable Defibrillators and Bi-Ventricular Pacemakers. Implantable Defibrillators are designed to detect and correct life-threatening arrhythmias by delivering electric shocks to restore a normal heartbeat. In contrast, Bi-Ventricular Pacemakers are utilized to coordinate the heart's pumping action by stimulating both ventricles, improving heart efficiency and symptoms in patients with heart failure. These devices play crucial roles in managing cardiac rhythm disorders, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
