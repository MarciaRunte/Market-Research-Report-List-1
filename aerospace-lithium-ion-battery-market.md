<p><p>The aerospace lithium-ion battery market comprises several types, each with unique characteristics. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LCO) is known for its high energy density but limited thermal stability. Lithium Titanate Oxide (LTO) excels in fast charging and longevity. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LMO) balances cost and performance, and Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminium Oxide (NCA) delivers high energy density and power efficiency, making it suitable for aerospace applications.</p></p>
