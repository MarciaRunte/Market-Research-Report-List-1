<p><p>The Aircraft Drive Shaft and Couplings market encompasses various components that transmit power between different parts of an aircraft. Universal joints allow for angular movement and alignment flexibility while transmitting torque. Oldham couplings accommodate misalignments by using sliding surfaces. Flexible shafts are designed to handle variations in distance and alignment, absorbing shocks and vibrations. Other market types include rigid couplings and specialized connectors. These components play a crucial role in ensuring efficient power transfer and operational reliability in aircraft systems.</p></p>
