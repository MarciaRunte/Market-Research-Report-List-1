<p><p>The Baggage Transport and Sortation System market is primarily segmented into two types: RFID and Barcode systems. RFID Baggage Transport Systems utilize radio frequency identification technology to enable automatic tracking and sorting of luggage, providing enhanced accuracy and efficiency. In contrast, Barcode Baggage Transport Systems rely on printed barcodes scanned to track baggage, making them cost-effective and widely adopted. Both systems improve operational efficiency and reduce lost luggage incidents in airports, but RFID offers faster processing capabilities compared to traditional barcoding.</p></p>
