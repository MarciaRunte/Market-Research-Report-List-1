<p><ul><li>High Ammonia Latex Concentrate</li><li>Low Ammonia Latex Concentrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concentrated latex market is primarily categorized into High Ammonia Latex Concentrate (HALC) and Low Ammonia Latex Concentrate (LALC). HALC contains a higher percentage of ammonia, which enhances stability and shelf-life, making it suitable for applications like rubber products and gloves. Conversely, LALC has reduced ammonia content, resulting in lower odor and improved sensitivity, ideal for applications in medical gloves and food packaging. These differing compositions cater to specific industrial needs while affecting product characteristics and performance.</p></p>
