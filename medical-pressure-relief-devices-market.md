<p><p>The medical pressure relief devices market comprises three primary types: mattresses, specialty beds, and mattress overlays. Mattresses are designed to reduce pressure on patients, enhancing comfort and preventing sores. Specialty beds include advanced systems, such as air-fluidized or low-air-loss beds, which cater to specific patient needs, such as those with severe mobility issues. Mattress overlays are additional cushioning layers placed on existing beds to provide enhanced pressure relief, making them cost-effective alternatives for improving patient comfort and preventing skin breakdown.</p></p>
