<p><strong>The Thyristor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Thyristor</li><li>Bidirectional Thyristor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thyristor market is primarily divided into two types: unidirectional thyristors and bidirectional thyristors. Unidirectional thyristors conduct current in one direction and are commonly used in applications like rectifiers and motor control. Bidirectional thyristors, on the other hand, allow current flow in both directions, making them suitable for AC applications such as light dimmers and temperature controllers. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the electrical system, influencing market demand and technological advancements.</p></p>
