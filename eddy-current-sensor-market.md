<p><p>Eddy current sensors are divided into two primary market types: split type and integrated type. Split type sensors consist of a separate sensing element and processing unit, allowing for flexibility in installation and maintenance, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, integrated type sensors combine the sensing element and processing circuitry in a single unit, offering compactness and ease of use. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to different requirements in automation, manufacturing, and material identification industries.</p></p>
