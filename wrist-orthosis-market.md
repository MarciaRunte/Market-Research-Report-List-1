<p><p>The wrist orthosis market features two primary types: ordinary splint type and inflatable type. Ordinary splint types are rigid and provide stable support for wrist injuries, promoting immobilization and healing. They are often made from materials like thermoplastics or foam. In contrast, inflatable types offer adjustable compression and support, using air chambers for customization. This flexibility allows users to modify the level of stabilization they need, making them suitable for both rehabilitation and preventive care in dynamic environments.</p></p>
