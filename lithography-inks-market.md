<p><p>The lithography inks market is primarily divided into three types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and others. Water-based inks are eco-friendly, drying quickly and providing vibrant colors, making them popular in commercial printing. Solvent-based inks, known for their versatility and durability, are typically used for outdoor applications due to their resistance to fading.
