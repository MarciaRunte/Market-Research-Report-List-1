<p><p>The industrial gas purifier market is primarily divided into two types: point of use gas purifiers and bulk gas purifiers. Point of use gas purifiers are designed for localized applications, providing purified gas directly at the consumption point, ensuring high purity levels for specific processes. In contrast, bulk gas purifiers cater to larger volumes and are typically used in centralized systems, purifying gas at scale for various industrial applications. Both serve critical roles in ensuring gas quality and operational efficiency.</p></p>
