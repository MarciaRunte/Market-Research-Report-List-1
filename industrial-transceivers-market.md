<p><strong>The Industrial Transceivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multi-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial transceivers market is categorized into single-mode and multi-mode types. Single-mode transceivers transmit signals over long distances using a single light path, making them ideal for applications requiring high data rates and minimal signal loss, such as telecommunications. In contrast, multi-mode transceivers utilize multiple light paths, allowing for shorter-range connections with higher data throughput, suitable for local area networks and within-building applications. Each type serves distinct networking needs based on distance, data rate, and cost.</p></p>
