<p><p>Powder polyester resins are thermosetting resins typically used in coatings, adhesives, and composites, characterized by their excellent chemical resistance, durability, and ease of application. The expansion of the construction and automotive sectors, coupled with rising awareness about sustainable products, further propels market growth. <p><ul><li>Hydroxyl Terminated</li><li>Carboxyl Terminated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Powder Polyester Resins market features two primary types: Hydroxyl Terminated and Carboxyl Terminated resins. Hydroxyl Terminated resins are characterized by functional hydroxyl groups, enhancing flexibility and adhesion in coatings and adhesives. In contrast, Carboxyl Terminated resins contain carboxylic acid groups, which improve cross-linking and chemical resistance, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Both types are widely utilized in powder coatings, providing durability and excellent finish while meeting specific performance requirements across various industries.</p></p>
